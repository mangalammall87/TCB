The Car Bros Origin

Origins in England, 1700s One of the first Car Deluxe Showrooms may have been Bennett's in Derby, first established as an WheelMonger (Car shop) in 2ND October 1885.The Showroom Was First Founded By Mr.Graeme Rudiger & Mr.David Kroos Along With Directors Mr.Lionel Mbappe, Mrs.Lindia Smith & Mrs.Geeta Kapoor.Now The Name Of Showrrom Was Then Tranformed To "The Car Guys" In 2000 By Then Owner Mr.Steve Kroos & Mrs.Victoria Rudiger. It still stands to this day, trading in the same building. However, the first reliably dated car store to be established, was The Car Bros & Co., which opened in 1955 on Pall Mall, Manchester. (The oldest Luxury Car store chain may be In Manchester.We Are One Of The Most Oldest car retailers in the world.With Above 137 years of our experience, we have now partnered with various world famous and reknowned car makers i.e Lamborghini,BMW,Audi,Porsche etc.We Have Already Sold Over 5000000 Cars All over The Globe.We Also have our branch in India At Mumbai.With Our Growing Business We Are Now Opening Our Branch In The Nation Of AutoMotors I.e Germany Soon.Today This World Class Showroom Has It's Ownership In The Hand Of Our Great Leader Mr.Marnus Rudiger.
